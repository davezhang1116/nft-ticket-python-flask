Mint NFT Ticket

What is this

You can create an ERC-721 token that can be used as tickets. They can be checked in like physical tickets. You can also make money on these tickets by setting up a price.

Functions

Deploy Your contract

You can create an ERC-721 token contract by connecting your Metamask wallet.

Pay To Mint Token

Users can mint token here by providing a token contract and the corresponding evm network.

List Your Tokens Of A Collection

Users can check their tokens by providing a token contract and the corresponding evm network.

Withdraw Your Sale (Only Owner)

Owner can withdraw their revenue by providing a token contract and the corresponding evm network.
